# River

## Appearance
- Shallow forest stream with blue-green water, gentle current visible
- Soft earthy banks where water meets ground, with occasional pebbles or reeds
- Water is slightly reflective, lighter in the center, darker at the banks
- Banks blend naturally into the surrounding green ground area

## Feature Fill (Blue Mask Region)
Fill with flowing water — the current should suggest movement along the connection direction. For corners and junctions, the water should curve or merge naturally.

## Ground Fill (Green Mask Region)
Fill with the same lush forest floor grass used in the base grass tile — short green grass with subtle variation.
