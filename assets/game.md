# Game World

## Setting
Fantasy forest world — ancient woods, winding rivers, mossy ruins, and quiet meadows.

## Palette
Warm greens, mossy earth tones, dappled golden light. Muted but saturated — not cartoonishly bright, not desaturated.

## Light
Light source from upper-left, shadows cast toward lower-right.

## Projection
True isometric, 2:1 width-to-height diamond ratio on the ground plane.
